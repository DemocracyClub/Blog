Who Can I Vote For?

{% filter markdown %} People expect information about elections and candidates to be available online. >I am looking for information on the candidates I am supposed to be voting for on Thursday. I cannot find anything on either candidate. If they cannot be bothered to get the information out there why should I bother turning out to vote??? We are making this happen: users put in their postcode and we show them everything we know. Ideally, this information includes a statement from the candidate, their photo and contact details. The website saw 500,000 uses at the last set of local elections. It now covers all by-elections too. It’s powered by our election data and our crowdsourced database of candidates. You can try it out at WhoCanIVoteFor.co.uk. Got suggestions for improvements? Find the site on Github We can only keep the site going — and make improvements — if you chip in. If you believe everybody should have the information they need to make an informed democratic decision, please support our work. Donate here. {% endfilter %}
