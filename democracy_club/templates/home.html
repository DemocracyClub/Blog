Democracy Club

Democracy Club is a group of volunteers that aims to increase the quantity, quality and accessibility of information on election candidates, politicians and democratic processes through digital tools, microvolunteering and collaboration with like-minded organisations.

Join our mailing list

Some of the ways you can help Democracy Club

Find information
on your candidates

Use YourNextMP.com to find out who your candidates are. If we don't currently hold all the information in our crowd sourced database then help out by finding a source and adding it to the site.

More details

Meet your candidates

Use MeetYourNextMP.com to find out what events your candidates are going to, so you can talk to them in person, or list events you know they will be attending if we don't already have them in our database.

More details

Read your
Candidate's CV

Use MPCV to read the CV of your candidates, or ask them to make it public so you can decide if you should give them a job.

More details

Read articles about your candidate or area

Election Mentions presents articles that we think are relevant in an attractive and simple list, facilitating further research, and ultimately helping to inform your vote. You can also add articles you find for other people to read.

More details

Contribute to code

We have some fun projects to work on! Everything we do is open source and we're always in need of technical people of any sort!

More details

Find your polling station

We're launching a hugely ambitious website that is trying to gather all the data on the location of polling stations all over the country so we can tell you where to vote. Help us get it all!

More details

Attend an event

We hold twice weekly events in London so if you're in the area come along and see how you can help us in the run up to the election.

More details

Consume our data

We have data. Lots of data. From 1,000s of election leaflets, to all the candidates, hustings and news articles. Take a look at our APIs page for more.
